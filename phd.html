Primary School Program (1st to 5th Grade) - RBSE


The Primary School Program is designed for young learners, providing them with a solid foundation in essential subjects such as language, mathematics, and environmental studies. This stage of learning focuses on nurturing curiosity, creativity, and the basic skills necessary for future education. The program comprises grade-appropriate coursework, with an emphasis on building fundamental literacy, numeracy, and awareness of the world around them.

Key Features

1. Language Development

The program places a strong emphasis on developing reading, writing, and speaking skills. Through stories, poems, and language exercises, students learn to express themselves clearly and confidently. Emphasis is placed on the use of Hindi and English, helping students build bilingual communication skills.

2. Mathematics Foundation

Students are introduced to basic mathematical concepts such as numbers, counting, addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. The program includes hands-on activities and exercises that make learning math fun and interactive, helping children develop logical thinking and problem-solving skills.

3. Environmental Studies

The curriculum introduces students to the world around them, focusing on their immediate environment, natural resources, and the importance of hygiene, health, and sustainability. Students learn through observation, exploration, and simple experiments, fostering a sense of responsibility towards nature.

4. Arts, Craft, and Creativity

Students engage in arts and crafts activities to develop their creativity and fine motor skills. Drawing, coloring, and other hands-on projects are integrated into the learning process, helping students express themselves artistically.

5. Moral and Social Development

The program also emphasizes values such as respect, kindness, and cooperation. Through stories, group activities, and classroom interactions, students learn important social skills and develop positive relationships with peers and teachers.
