Careers @ Samrpit Career Institute


  • Faculty

    PSamrpit Career Institute, Churu, Rajasthan, invites applications from qualified and passionate educators to join our team and contribute to shaping the future of students from Classes 1 to 12. We are looking for motivated teachers with expertise in Mathematics, Science, English, Hindi, Social Studies, and other subjects as per the RBSE syllabus.

    • We have openings for the following positions:

    1. School Teachers (Classes 1-5)

    2. Middle School Teachers (Classes 6-8)

    3. Senior Secondary Teachers (Classes 9-12)

    3. Subject Experts for Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), Mathematics, and English at the higher secondary level.

    Candidates must hold relevant qualifications such as a B.Ed. or M.Ed., and a deep understanding of their subject area. Prior experience in teaching RBSE students is preferred.

    To apply, please submit the following:

    1. Curriculum vitae (CV)

    2. A brief statement of your teaching philosophy

    3. List of subjects taught and relevant experience

    4. References (with contact details)
