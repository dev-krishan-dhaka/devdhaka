FAQs

Samrpit Career Institute is a premier school located in Churu, Rajasthan, offering education from 1st to 12th grades in both Hindi and English mediums. The school is committed to providing quality education and preparing students for RBSE board exams and various competitive exams like NEET and JEE.


How do I get admission to Samrpit Career Institute?

Admissions are open for all classes from 1st to 12th grade. There is no entrance exam, and seats are allotted based on first-come, first-served for the lower classes. For 11th and 12th grade, admissions are based on the student's previous academic performance. The institute offers streams in Science (both medical and non-medical), Commerce, and Arts.

What facilities does the school provide?

1.Hostel Facility:

The institute has a well-furnished hostel that can accommodate students from outstation. It is located near the school premises, ensuring convenience for the students.


2.Transport Facility:

School buses are available for students coming from nearby villages and towns.


3.Mess Facility:

A healthy and hygienic mess service is available for hostel students.

What are the roles of the faculty?

Our teachers are dedicated to providing quality education and ensuring that every student understands the subjects thoroughly. In addition to regular classes, teachers offer extra coaching for students needing additional help in various subjects, particularly in Math, Science, and English for higher classes.

What is the closing date for admissions?

Admissions are generally open until the end of June every year. However, seats are limited, and it is advised to apply as early as possible. Special counseling sessions for 11th and 12th-grade admissions start in April.
